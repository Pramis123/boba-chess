The History of Chess

Chess originated in northern India around the 6th century before spreading across Persia, the Islamic world, and eventually Europe. It has evolved into one of the most popular and respected strategy games in history.

Meet the Chess Pieces

King

The most important piece — protect it at all costs.

Queen

The most powerful piece with full mobility.

Rook

Moves in straight lines across ranks and files.

Bishop

Moves diagonally and covers long distances quickly.

Knight

Moves in an L‑shape and can jump over pieces.

Pawn

Small but mighty — capable of promotion.

Beginner Strategies

  • Control the center early.
  • Develop your pieces efficiently.
  • Castle to protect your king.
  • Avoid moving the same piece repeatedly.
